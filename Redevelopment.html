About Our Redevelopment Projects

As times change and lifestyles evolve, people desire homes that live up to this transformation. Understanding this specific need of Punekars, Shree Omkareshwar Developers initiated redevelopment of some of the oldest addresses at Pune’s most popular locations.

Taking care of people’s cherished emotions about their homes, inherited by them across generations, we follow a transparent and hassle-free redevelopment process to deliver the best space and lifestyle upgrades.

Need for Redevelopment in a Society

  • Structural Safety – Replaces old and weak buildings with earthquake-resistant and durable structures.
  • Regulatory Compliance – Aligns with current building codes, fire safety norms, and municipal regulations.
  • Modern Amenities – Provides lifts, parking, security systems, green spaces, and other lifestyle facilities.
  • Optimized Land Utilization – Ensures efficient use of space, offering larger and better-planned apartments.
  • Enhanced Property Value – Increases market worth and rental potential of flats.
  • Reduced Maintenance Costs – Eliminates recurring expenses on repairs and structural upkeep.
  • Improved Quality of Life – Creates a sustainable, comfortable, and community-friendly living environment.

Our Redevelopment Process

Step 1

The first step is to understand the needs of the residents and study the plot. We follow it up with a brief presentation about the redevelopment overview. This includes the layout, draft plans, commercial terms, timelines, specifications, shifting arrangements and document requirements.

Step 2

The project plan is reworked to fit-in the requirements of the members. A final draft of the techno-commercial plan, with building sketches and legal agreements is presented to the committee. This is followed by obtaining sanctions from the municipal/local authority.

Step 3

Once commercial terms have been fulfilled, the residents are shifted into rented apartments. The building is thus ready to be handed over for redevelopment. The process of redevelopment will be initiated and completed as per the sanctioned plan.

Step 4

Once the building is ready as per the architectural assessment, aspects such as finishing, inspection and various other installations will be looked into thoroughly. The occupation as well as completion certificate gets cleared and the building is handed over to the residents with respective inductions.
